MASTERING THE ART OF CREATING LUXURY APPAREL.

Our goal is to continue developing our unique technological systems in order to further enhance our capabilities and deliver our designs to each beautiful woman looking to wear a garment created for her and only her.

  • Over the past three years, Andrew's Bridals has established itself as one of the leading couture fashion houses, with garments characterized by their high-end fabrications, meticulous sewing techniques, and of course, our unique technology which enables us to make each garment meet the wearer’s exact measurements from overseas
  • Our emphasis on personalization and customization, alongside our uncompromising designs, is what continues to differentiate us from other designers.
  • Our couture bridal gowns are known for their romantic and sensual nature and exquisite details, along with their figure-flattering silhouettes and emphasis on comfort. Andrew's Bridals gowns define new standards for high fashion and our models are always ahead of their time.

Andrew's Bridals pursues excellence in design and irreproachable craftsmanship to create a gown worthy of a bride’s most treasured moments. We believe brides should feel nothing less than gorgeous on their wedding day. Our designs blend rich fabrics like satin and lace with decadent beading and detailing, in addition to the comfort of our patented Andrew's Bridals construction. The result is the Andrew's Bridals brand family, with collections that embody the unique style of our brides.

Gowns

Get Your Special Gown With Us

Ball Gown

Ksh.70000

What to know about the Ball Gown

  • The bodice of a ball gown is typically fitted to the waist, providing a structured and slimming look, and often features a corset-like closure for extra support.
  • The full skirt of a ball gown can be flattering on many body types, as it can create the illusion of curves and balance out proportions.
  • Comes with different necklines such as V-neck, scoop, bateau, or off-the-shoulder, providing options for different preferences and dress codes.

Ball gowns also have different sleeve options like sleeveless, short sleeves, cap sleeves, long sleeves, and off-the-shoulder which give a variety of options.

A-Line

Ksh.20000

What to know about A-Line

  • Flared skirt that gradually widens from the waist to the hem, creating the shape of an "A" and giving the dress a full and flowing appearance.
  • The bodice of an A-line gown is typically fitted to the waist, providing a structured and slimming look.
  • The flare of the skirt can balance out proportions and create a flattering silhouette for many body types, making it a popular choice among women.

A-line gowns also have different sleeve options like sleeveless, short sleeves, cap sleeves, long sleeves and off-the-shoulder which give a variety of options.

Mermaid

Ksh.60000

What to know about Mermaid Gown

  • Fitted silhouette that tightly hugs the body from the bodice to the knee before flaring out into a dramatic skirt, creating a unique and dramatic look.
  • The gown is designed to accentuate the curves of the body, particularly the hips and thighs, creating a feminine and alluring look.
  • The fitted silhouette of the mermaid gown is typically best suited for slim and hourglass body types, as the gown can emphasize any areas of the body that are not proportionate.

Also have different sleeve options like sleeveless, short sleeves, cap sleeves, long sleeves and off-the-shoulder which give a variety of options.
